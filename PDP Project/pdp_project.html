What Is Pycnogenol?

Life Extension’s Pycnogenol® helps your body fight back against the normal aging process by inhibiting inflammatory factors and oxidative stress to support overall health, promoting healthy circulation and blood vessel function and maintaining healthy glucose metabolism.

Pycnogenol® contains procyanidins, flavonoids and other beneficial compounds that help your body fight the effects of aging.

Pycnogenol® Benefits

  • Supports vascular health & healthy circulation
  • Promotes a healthy inflammatory respons
  • Helps maintain already-healthy glucose level
  • Provides powerful antioxidant protection

Antioxidant Defenses

Pycnogenol® bolsters your body’s antioxidant defenses, inhibiting the free-radical activity that can affect cells, tissue, organs and even DNA. Pycnogenol® also supports healthy glucose metabolism and has showed it can help maintain already-healthy blood sugar levels.

Product Details

Vascular health & circulation

Pycnogenol® supports endothelial and arterial health by helping to inhibit inflammatory factors called cytokines.
